Privacy Policy

How we protect your information

Information We Collect

When you purchase something from our boutique, as part of the buying and selling process, we collect personal information you give us such as your name, address, and email address to fulfill your order and provide shipping updates.

Payment Security

We use Razorpay for processing payments. We/Razorpay do not store your card data on their servers. The data is encrypted through the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI-DSS) when processing payment. Your purchase transaction data is only used as long as is necessary to complete your purchase transaction.

Third-Party Services

In general, the third-party providers used by us (such as shipping partners) will only collect, use, and disclose your information to the extent necessary to allow them to perform the services they provide to us.

Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your shopping cart session and provide a seamless browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.
