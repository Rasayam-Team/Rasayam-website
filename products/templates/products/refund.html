Exchange & Returns

Our commitment to your satisfaction

Order Cancellations

Because we begin processing orders promptly to ensure quick delivery, cancellations are only accepted within 24 hours of placing the order. Once an order has been dispatched from our atelier, it cannot be cancelled.

Return Window

We accept returns and exchanges within 7 days of the delivery date. To be eligible for a return, the garment must be unused, unwashed, and in the exact same condition that you received it, with all original tags securely attached.

Non-Returnable Items

To maintain the exclusivity and hygiene of our premium ethnic wear, custom-fitted or bespoke items are not eligible for return or exchange unless there is a manufacturing defect.

Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected by our quality team, we will send you an email to notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, the refund will be processed and automatically credited back to your original method of payment (via Razorpay) within 5 to 7 business days.
