Shipping & Delivery

From our atelier to your doorstep

Processing Time

Each RASAYAM garment undergoes a rigorous quality check. Orders are typically processed and dispatched within 2 to 4 business days of successful payment.

Delivery Estimates

We partner with premium courier services to ensure safe transit. Standard delivery across India takes approximately 5 to 7 business days following dispatch.

Shipping Charges

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location. We may offer complimentary shipping on orders exceeding a specific value, as indicated on the website.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, a tracking link will be sent to your registered email and mobile number, allowing you to monitor your garment's journey in real-time.
